Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2, 2025

Load Swap is a standalone offline utility application designed for converting measurement units of weight We prioritize your privacy and are committed to protecting any information you may provide. In this case, our app is specifically engineered so that it does not require, collect, or transmit any personal or sensitive information at all.

1. Information We Collect

Load Swap does not collect or store any personal data. We do not require user registration, login credentials, or access to personal or sensitive information.

1.1 Usage Data

We do not collect any analytics or usage data. All operations, including weight conversions, are performed entirely offline on the user’s device.

1.2 Permissions

The App does not request access to your contacts, camera, location, storage, microphone, or any other device permissions. It is fully functional without internet access or system integration.

2. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose any user information to third parties. Since no personal data is collected, there is nothing to share or disclose.

3. Internet and Network Use

Load Swap functions entirely offline. It does not access or transmit data over the internet, and no data is sent to external servers. The app does not include any advertisements or third-party services.

4. Data Security

As the App does not store or process any user data, no information is at risk of breach or unauthorized access. The app is designed with privacy in mind by default.

5. Children's Privacy

Load Swap is suitable for users of all ages, including children. It does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13, and complies with all applicable child protection laws such as COPPA.
